Popular Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our AI agents.

An AI agent is a pre-built software program designed to perform specific tasks automatically, such as answering customer queries, managing social media, or generating content. Think of it as a specialized digital employee that you can 'hire' instantly from our store.

After you purchase an agent, you gain access to our intuitive, no-code Customization Studio. From there, you can easily adjust the agent's personality, tone of voice, knowledge base, and specific workflows to perfectly match your brand and business processes. No coding is required.

A one-time purchase gives you a lifetime license to the agent as it exists at the time of purchase. A subscription provides continuous value by including ongoing training, performance updates to keep up with the latest AI advancements, and access to our premium support team.

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee on all our AI agent purchases. If you are not satisfied with the product for any reason, you can request a full refund within 14 days of your purchase. Please see our Terms of Use for full details.

Integration is simple. For most agents, like website chatbots, we provide a small snippet of code that you can easily copy and paste into your website's HTML. For other integrations, we provide step-by-step guides and an API for developers who need deeper system connections.
