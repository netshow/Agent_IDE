Disclosure

Our commitment to transparency in our business practices.

At AI Agent Factory Online, we believe that transparency is essential for building trust with our users. This page discloses information about our business model and how we operate.

Business Model

AI Agent Factory Online is a Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) company. We generate revenue through monthly and annual subscription fees for access to our platform. Our pricing tiers (e.g., Free, Basic, Pro, Enterprise) offer different levels of features, usage limits, and support. This subscription-based model allows us to continuously invest in improving our platform, adding new features, and providing excellent customer support.

Affiliate Relationships

In some cases, we may participate in affiliate marketing programs. This means that if you click on a link to a third-party service that we recommend (for example, through our Zapier integration) and make a purchase, we may receive a small commission at no extra cost to you. We only recommend products and services that we believe will add value to our users. Our editorial content is not influenced by these affiliate partnerships.

Editorial Independence

The content on our blog and in our educational materials is created to provide helpful, unbiased information to our users. Our content is based on our team's expertise and research and is not influenced by advertisers or affiliate partners. Our primary goal is to educate and empower our users to succeed with AI automation.
