Our Platform Services

A suite of powerful services and features that form the core of our AI Agent Factory.

No-Code/Low-Code Agent Builder

Our primary service is providing access to an intuitive agent builder that requires no programming knowledge. If you can map out a workflow on a whiteboard, you can build a powerful AI agent. This service empowers marketers, support leads, and business owners to create automation without relying on development teams.

Industry-Specific Template Library

We provide a constantly growing library of pre-built agent templates as a service to our users. This accelerates development time dramatically. Simply select a template for your industry (e.g., e-commerce, healthcare), customize it with your branding and data, and launch.

Managed Integration Service

Our platform offers a managed integration service, featuring one-click connectors to popular CRMs, e-commerce platforms, and communication tools. We handle the complexities of API connections, so you can focus on your agent's logic. Our Zapier integration further connects you to thousands of apps.

Multi-Channel Deployment & Hosting

We provide a comprehensive deployment and hosting service. Once your agent is built, you can deploy it across multiple channels simultaneously with just a few clicks. We manage the cloud infrastructure, ensuring your agent is always available, secure, and scalable to meet demand.

AI Training & Knowledge Management

Train your AI agent to become an expert in your business. Our service includes a user-friendly dashboard where you can upload documents, add FAQs, and connect to live data sources. We manage the underlying AI models that learn from this data to provide accurate and context-aware responses.

Analytics and Optimization Suite

Our platform includes a full suite of analytics tools as a service. You can monitor your agent's performance in real-time, track key metrics, and gather insights to continuously improve its effectiveness. This data-driven approach ensures you get the maximum return on your automation investment.
