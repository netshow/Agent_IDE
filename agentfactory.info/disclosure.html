Disclosure Policy

Our commitment to editorial independence and transparency.

Last Updated: [Date]

Agent Factory Info is committed to providing unbiased, accurate, and trustworthy information. This policy outlines our business model and editorial standards.

1. Editorial Independence

Our primary commitment is to our readers. All of our educational content, including articles, tutorials, and research summaries, is produced with strict editorial independence. Our analysis and conclusions are not for sale and are not influenced by advertisers or partners.

2. Business Model

We generate revenue through several channels to support our mission:

  • Premium Subscriptions: Access to our most in-depth content, reports, and our full knowledge base is available via a paid subscription.
  • Courses & Certifications: We sell access to our expert-led educational courses and professional certification programs.
  • Advertising & Sponsored Content: We may feature advertising or sponsored articles on our site. All such content will be clearly and conspicuously marked as "Advertisement" or "Sponsored" to distinguish it from our editorial content.
  • Expert Directory Listings: Experts and companies pay a fee to be listed in our directory. All listings are vetted for relevance and expertise, but a listing does not constitute an editorial endorsement.
  • Affiliate Links: We may use affiliate links when recommending tools or services. If you make a purchase through these links, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. Our recommendations are always based on our honest assessment of the product's quality and relevance.
