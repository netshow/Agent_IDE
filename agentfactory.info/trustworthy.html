Trust is the foundation of education and information. Here is our pledge to you.

Our Trustworthiness Pledge

Our entire platform is built on the promise of providing trustworthy information. We are committed to upholding that promise in everything we do.

1. Honesty and Transparency

We are always upfront about how we operate. Our business model and editorial standards are clearly explained in our Disclosure Policy. We clearly distinguish between editorial content and advertising, so you always know the nature of the information you are consuming.

2. A Mission to Inform, Not to Sell

Our primary mission is to educate and inform. We do not sell AI agent software, which makes us a uniquely trustworthy resource. Our success is measured by your learning and enlightenment, not by a sales quota.

3. Accountability and Corrections

While we strive for perfection, we are human. If we make an error, we will correct it promptly and transparently. We are accountable to our readers and are always open to feedback to improve the accuracy of our content.

4. Respect for Your Privacy

We respect your privacy. We only collect the data necessary to provide our services, and we will never sell your personal information. Our Privacy Policy details our commitment to protecting your data.
