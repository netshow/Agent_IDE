Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting the privacy of our readers and subscribers.

Agent Factory Info ("us", "we", or "our") operates the agentfactory.info website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide and improve our Service. This includes:

  • For Newsletter Subscribers: Your email address.
  • For Premium Subscribers & Course Students: Your name, email address, and payment information (processed securely by a third-party payment provider like Stripe).
  • For All Visitors: Anonymized usage data and analytics (e.g., page views, time on site) collected via standard tools like Google Analytics to help us understand what content is most valuable to our audience.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the purpose for which it was provided:

  • To send you our newsletter if you subscribed.
  • To manage your premium subscription and provide access to paid content and courses.
  • To analyze and improve our content and user experience.

We will never sell your personal information to third parties.

3. Cookies

We use cookies to ensure a smooth user experience, such as keeping you logged into your account and understanding site traffic. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

4. Security

We take the security of your data seriously and use industry-standard measures to protect it. All payment information is handled by our secure, PCI-compliant payment processor, and is never stored on our servers.
