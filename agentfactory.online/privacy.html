Privacy Policy

Our commitment to protecting your data and your customers' data.

Last Updated: [Date]

Agent Factory Online ("us", "we", or "our") operates the agentfactory.online platform (the "Service"). This policy details our commitment to data privacy for our users and their end-users.

1. User Data

When you sign up for our Service, we collect information such as your name, email, and company details. This is used to manage your account, provide customer support, and for billing purposes. We will never sell your personal data.

2. Agent & Conversation Data

The agents you build and the conversation data generated between your agents and your end-users are your property. We process this data on your behalf to provide the Service. We use this data for:

  • Service Provision: To operate your agent and display conversation logs and analytics on your dashboard.
  • System Improvement: We may use anonymized and aggregated conversation data to improve our underlying AI models and the overall performance of the platform. We will never use your specific, identifiable conversation data to train general models without your explicit consent.

3. Data Security

We implement robust, industry-standard security measures to protect all user and conversation data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes encryption in transit and at rest, strict access controls for our personnel, and regular security audits.

4. HIPAA and Data Compliance

For users on our Enterprise plan, we offer a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) to support HIPAA compliance. Our platform includes features designed to help you meet your regulatory obligations, but you are ultimately responsible for ensuring your agent's design and use case comply with all applicable laws.
