A Look Under the Hood

The core components that make building powerful agents easy.

The Visual Flow Builder

Our drag-and-drop canvas is the heart of the factory. Visually map out conversations, add conditions, connect actions, and see your agent's logic come to life in real-time. No code, just clarity.

The Template Library

Don't start from scratch. Our library contains dozens of pre-built agent templates for specific industries and use cases, from e-commerce product finders to real estate lead qualifiers. Just pick a template and customize it.

The Analytics Dashboard

Data-driven optimization is built-in. Track key metrics like conversation volume, user satisfaction, and goal completion rates. Our AI-powered "Optimizer" even suggests improvements to your conversational flows.

The Skills Marketplace

Extend your agent's capabilities instantly. Browse our marketplace to add powerful "skills" created by our community, such as advanced integrations with CRMs, payment gateways, and booking systems.
