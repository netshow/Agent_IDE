Our Vision, Mission & Story

Democratizing intelligent automation for every business.

Our Vision: Intelligent Automation for Every Business

At Agent Factory Online, we envision a future where every business, regardless of size or technical expertise, can leverage the power of intelligent automation. We see a world where routine tasks are handled by efficient AI agents, freeing up humans to focus on creativity, strategy, and building meaningful customer relationships.

Our Mission: To Be the World's Easiest and Most Powerful AI Agent Factory

Our mission is to democratize AI agent development. We are committed to building a platform that combines cutting-edge AI technology with radically simple design, enabling anyone to create, deploy, and manage highly effective virtual agents. We strive to provide the tools, resources, and ecosystem our users need to turn their automation ideas into reality, quickly and affordably.

Our Story

Agent Factory Online was founded in 2024 by a team of software developers and small business enthusiasts who saw a clear need for a true "factory"â€”a platform that provided the assembly line, the parts, and the instructions for anyone to produce a professional-grade AI agent. We focused on a no-code, visual approach and built an end-to-end ecosystem to support users from their first simple chatbot to a fleet of sophisticated, revenue-generating agents.

What Makes Agent Factory Online Unique?

  • The "Factory" Ecosystem: We provide the entire production ecosystem in one place: a no-code builder, industry blueprints, a skills marketplace, and powerful analytics.
  • Radical Simplicity: We are obsessed with making powerful technology easy to use. Our drag-and-drop interface means you can have a functional agent live in under an hour.
  • A Thriving Marketplace: Our skills marketplace fosters a powerful community effect, allowing you to add advanced capabilities to your agent instantly.

Our Team

Our team is a mix of AI engineers, UX designers, and business strategists who are passionate about helping small and medium-sized businesses succeed. We have deep experience building scalable cloud software and are committed to providing our users with a reliable platform and world-class support.
