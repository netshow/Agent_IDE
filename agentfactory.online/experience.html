Built from Real-World Experience

Our platform was born from our team's firsthand experience with the challenges small businesses face.

Solving a Problem We Know Well

Agent Factory Online wasn't designed in a corporate boardroom. It was designed by a team that has spent years working with and for small and medium-sized businesses. We've experienced the pain points of customer support overload, missed leads, and the struggle to compete with larger companies.

A Team with Relevant Experience

Our team's collective experience is perfectly suited to this mission:

  • Small Business Operations: Several members of our founding team have run their own e-commerce and service-based businesses. They know the day-to-day challenges and what a truly useful automation tool needs to do.
  • SaaS and Cloud Software Development: Our engineering team has decades of combined experience building scalable, reliable, and secure cloud-based software. They know how to build a professional-grade platform that our customers can depend on.
  • User Experience (UX) for Non-Technical Users: A core part of our experience is designing intuitive interfaces for non-technical audiences. We have a proven track record of making complex technology simple and accessible.

From Frustration to "Factory"

Our platform is the direct result of our experience watching business owners struggle with overly complex tools. We set out to build the solution we wished had existed for our friends and former colleagues: a simple, powerful "factory" where any business owner could assemble the virtual agents they need to grow.
