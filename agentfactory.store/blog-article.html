Case Study: How a Real Estate Agency Automated 80% of Their Lead Qualification

Posted on January 8, 2024 by The Content Team

Prime Properties, a boutique real estate agency, was facing a common problem: their agents were spending too much time on the phone with unqualified leads, while serious potential buyers were sometimes left waiting. They needed a way to engage every website visitor instantly and efficiently filter for the most promising prospects. They turned to Agent Factory Shop.

The Solution: The "Real Estate Lead Qualifier" Agent

Prime Properties purchased our pre-built Real Estate Lead Qualifier agent. In under an hour, using our no-code customization studio, they tailored the agent to their business:

  • They customized the conversational flow to match their brand's friendly yet professional tone.
  • They added their specific qualifying questions, such as "Are you pre-approved for a mortgage?" and "What is your desired move-in date?"
  • They connected the agent to their agents' Calendly accounts, allowing qualified leads to book a viewing directly.

The Results

The impact was immediate and dramatic. Within the first month:

  • The AI agent handled 100% of initial website inquiries.
  • 80% of leads were fully qualified (or disqualified) by the agent without any human involvement.
  • Their human agents were able to spend their time exclusively on high-intent, pre-qualified buyers, leading to a 35% increase in properties sold.

"The agent from Agent Factory Shop has transformed our business. Our team is happier and more productive, and our sales have never been better. It's the best investment we've made all year."

Susan Mills, Owner of Prime Properties
