A Platform You Can Trust

Trust is built on transparency, reliability, and putting our customers first.

Our Trustworthiness Pledge

When you build an agent on our platform, you're trusting us with a part of your business. We take that responsibility seriously and are committed to earning your trust every day.

1. We Protect Your Data (and Your Customers' Data)

Data security and privacy are our top priorities. We use industry-best practices to secure our platform, your account data, and the data from your agent's conversations. Our Privacy Policy is clear: you own your data, and we are simply its steward.

2. A Reliable Service You Can Count On

Your agent is often the first point of contact for your customers. We understand that it needs to be online and working 24/7. We invest heavily in our infrastructure to ensure high uptime and performance, so you can trust your agent to always be on the job.

3. Transparent and Honest Business Practices

We believe in being upfront with our customers. Our pricing is simple and transparent. Our marketing is honest. We would rather lose a sale than mislead a customer about our platform's capabilities.

4. Our Success Is Your Success

Our business model is based on long-term partnerships with our customers. We succeed when your agents succeed at driving growth and improving efficiency for your business. This alignment means we are always motivated to help you get the best possible results from our platform.
