Product Details: The No-Code Agent Builder

A closer look at the heart of our platform, where your AI agents come to life.

Intuitive Visual Design

The No-Code Agent Builder is our flagship product, designed to empower every member of your team. It replaces complex coding with a simple, visual, drag-and-drop interface. You can map out entire conversations, define complex decision logic, and set up automated tasks as easily as creating a flowchart.

  • Drag-and-Drop Canvas: Visually construct your agent's workflow.
  • Conditional Logic: Create smart agents that react differently based on user input.
  • Pre-built Action Blocks: Use ready-made blocks for sending emails, updating CRMs, and more.

Low-Code for Professionals

While the builder is no-code at its core, it's also designed for professionals who need more power. For developers or technical users, the builder provides low-code escape hatches and API access to extend its capabilities beyond the visual interface.

  • Custom API Calls: Integrate with any internal or third-party service with a REST API.
  • Embeddable Scripts: Inject small scripts for custom data manipulation or calculations.
  • SDK Access: Utilize our Software Development Kits for full programmatic control.
