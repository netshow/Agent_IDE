Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to our professional relationship.

Last Updated: October 27, 2023

AI Agent Factory Pro ("us", "we", or "our") operates the aiagentfactory.pro website (the "Service"). This page informs you of our robust policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service and the choices you have associated with that data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect information necessary to provide and improve our professional Service to you. This includes account information (name, email, company), payment information for subscriptions, and usage data to enhance platform performance.

2. Use of Your Business Data

The data you upload to train your AI agents, including documents, FAQs, and integrations with your CRM or other systems ("Training Data"), is considered your confidential and proprietary information.

  • Your Training Data is used exclusively for the operation and improvement of YOUR specific AI agents.
  • We do not use your proprietary Training Data to train our general AI models or for the benefit of any other customer.
  • Access to your Training Data by our personnel is strictly limited to support and troubleshooting purposes, and only with your consent where applicable.

3. Data Security and Compliance

As a professional service, we implement enterprise-grade security measures. This includes encryption of data at rest and in transit, regular security audits, and strict internal access controls. Our platform is designed to be compliant with GDPR and CCPA, and we provide tools and agreements to support our customers' HIPAA compliance requirements.

4. Data Retention

We retain your Personal Data only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy. We will retain and use your data to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our legal agreements and policies.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

AI Agent Factory Pro aims to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data. As a professional user, you have the right to access, rectification, objection to processing, and data portability, among others, in accordance with applicable laws.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact our support team.
