Our Platform Products

Explore the core modules that make up the AI Agent Factory Pro platform.

AI Agent Factory Pro is a suite of interconnected products, each designed to handle a critical part of the AI agent lifecycle, from creation to analysis.

No-Code Agent Builder

Our flagship product. An intuitive, drag-and-drop visual interface where you design conversational flows, define automated tasks, and integrate AI capabilities. This is the "factory floor" where your agents are assembled.

Template Marketplace

A library of pre-built, industry-specific AI agents. Choose a template for e-commerce, healthcare, or finance, and get a fully functional agent that you can customize and launch in minutes, dramatically reducing development time.

Integration Hub

A suite of one-click connectors that link your agents to the tools you use every day. This product includes native integrations for platforms like Salesforce and Zendesk, as well as a universal Zapier connector for thousands of other apps.

AI Training Dashboard

This module is where you make your agent an expert. Upload your business documents, FAQs, and product catalogs. The Training Dashboard processes this information to ensure your agent provides accurate, context-aware, and on-brand responses.

Multi-Channel Deployment Center

A centralized hub for launching your agent across all your customer touchpoints. With a few clicks, you can deploy the same agent to your website, mobile app, social media channels, and more, ensuring a consistent experience everywhere.

Analytics & ROI Dashboard

This product provides actionable insights into your agent's performance. Monitor user interactions, track conversion rates, measure customer satisfaction, and identify the tangible return on investment (ROI) your agents are generating.
