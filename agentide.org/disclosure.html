Disclosure Policy

Our commitment to transparency in our non-profit, open-source project.

Last Updated: [Date]

Agent IDE Org is a non-profit organization dedicated to building open-source tools for the AI community. This policy outlines our operational transparency.

1. Open Source and Community Driven

Our primary product is our open-source IDE, licensed under the [e.g., Apache 2.0 License]. All development, discussions, and decision-making for the open-source project occur in public on our community channels like GitHub and Discord. We are funded by a combination of grants, donations, and revenue from our optional, managed cloud services.

2. Financial Transparency

As a non-profit organization, we are committed to financial transparency. Our annual financial reports, detailing our sources of funding and expenditures, are available upon request to ensure accountability to our community and donors.

3. Partnerships and Sponsors

We partner with corporations and academic institutions that support our mission. Any significant financial contributions or sponsorships will be publicly acknowledged. These partnerships do not give sponsors undue influence over the direction of the open-source project, which is governed by the core maintainers and the community.

4. Affiliate Links

Our blog and documentation may occasionally contain affiliate links to third-party services (e.g., cloud hosting providers, API services). If you make a purchase through these links, we may earn a commission that helps support our non-profit mission. All such links will be clearly disclosed.
