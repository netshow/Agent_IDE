A Trustworthy Partner for Your Ambition

Trust is the foundation of our Club. Here's our commitment to you.

Our Trustworthiness Pledge

As an exclusive community built on collaboration and commerce, we understand that trust is non-negotiable. We are committed to earning and keeping the trust of every member.

1. Protecting Your Intellectual Property

Your ideas and creations are your own. The work you do in your private lab space is confidential and secure. When you sell on our marketplace, you control the terms. We are a platform to empower you, not to exploit your IP. This is our most sacred promise.

2. A Fair and Transparent Marketplace

Our marketplace operates on clear and simple rules. Commission structures are transparent, and our review and rating systems are designed to promote fairness. We actively monitor for bad actors to maintain a high-trust environment for commerce.

3. Upholding Community Standards

We are committed to maintaining a respectful, professional, and collaborative atmosphere. Our community guidelines are strictly enforced to ensure that the Club remains a safe and productive space for all members to connect and grow.

4. Aligning Our Success with Yours

Our business model—based on membership fees and marketplace commissions—is fundamentally trustworthy because it aligns our success with yours. We only win when our members are so successful and get so much value from the community that they are happy to be a part of it. Your growth is our growth.
