Our Core Expertise

Specializing in the key areas that make AI simple and effective for SMBs.

Our platform is powerful because we focus our expertise on what matters most to small business owners: simplicity, reliability, and results.

Intuitive No-Code Interface Design

Our number one area of expertise is in user experience (UX) design for non-technical users. We specialize in making complex technology feel simple and intuitive through our drag-and-drop builder, clear labeling, and helpful guides.

Simple and Reliable Integrations

We are experts at creating hassle-free integrations. Our expertise ensures that connecting your AI agent to platforms like Shopify, WordPress, or your social media accounts is a straightforward process that works reliably without needing technical support.

SMB Industry Workflows

We specialize in understanding the common workflows and challenges of various small business industries. This expertise allows us to create agent templates (for retail, hospitality, services, etc.) that are genuinely useful and provide immediate value.

Applied AI for Practical Tasks

We don't focus on theoretical AI. Our expertise is in applying powerful AI models to solve practical, everyday business tasks like answering customer questions, generating marketing ideas, and scheduling appointments. We handle the complexity so you can enjoy the benefits.
