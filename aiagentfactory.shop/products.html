Our Platform Products

Explore the core modules you get with AI Agent Factory Shop.

AI Agent Factory Shop is a suite of interconnected products, each designed to handle a critical part of the AI agent lifecycle for your small business.

No-Code Agent Builder

Our flagship product. An intuitive, drag-and-drop visual interface where you design conversational flows, define automated tasks, and integrate AI capabilities. This is the "factory floor" where your agents are assembled.

Learn More →

Template Marketplace

A library of pre-built, industry-specific AI agents. Choose a template for e-commerce, cafes, or real estate, and get a fully functional agent that you can customize and launch in minutes.

Integration Hub

A suite of one-click connectors that link your agents to the tools you use every day. This product includes native integrations for platforms like Shopify and social media, plus a universal Zapier connector.

AI Training Dashboard

This module is where you make your agent an expert. Upload your business documents and FAQs. The Training Dashboard processes this information to ensure your agent provides accurate, on-brand responses.

Multi-Channel Deployment Center

A centralized hub for launching your agent across all your customer touchpoints. With a few clicks, you can deploy the same agent to your website, Facebook page, Instagram DMs, and more.

Analytics & ROI Dashboard

This product provides actionable insights into your agent's performance. Monitor user interactions, track leads generated, and measure the tangible return on investment (ROI) your agents are generating.
