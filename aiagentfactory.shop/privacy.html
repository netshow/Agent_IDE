Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. We keep it simple and clear.

Last Updated: October 27, 2023

AI Agent Factory Shop ("us", "we", or "our") operates the aiagentfactory.shop website (the "Service"). This policy explains how we handle your information in simple terms.

What We Collect

To use our service, we collect basic account information like your name, email address, and company name. When you subscribe, our payment processor handles your payment information securely.

Your Business Data is Yours

The information you upload to train your AI agent (like your FAQs or product lists) is your property. We only use this data to make YOUR agent work. We will never use your business data to train our main AI models or share it with other customers.

How We Use Your Information

We use your account information to manage your account, provide customer support, and send you important updates about the service. We use anonymous usage data to see how our platform is being used so we can make it better.

Data Security

We take your security seriously. We use standard security practices like encryption to protect your information. While no system is 100% foolproof, we do our best to keep your data safe.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your account information at any time. If you have any questions about your data, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any major changes to this policy, we will notify you by email or through a notice on our website.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
