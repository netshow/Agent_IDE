An Honest Look at Our Platform

Understanding the advantages and considerations for your small business.

Pros for Small Business

  • Extremely Affordable: Our pricing is designed specifically for SMB budgets, making powerful AI accessible for the first time.
  • Incredibly Easy to Use: The no-code builder and industry templates mean you can have an agent running in under an hour, with no technical skills needed.
  • Saves Valuable Time: By automating repetitive tasks like answering FAQs and scheduling, our platform gives you back hours every week to focus on growing your business.
  • Increases Sales and Leads: A 24/7 agent can capture leads, answer product questions, and reduce cart abandonment, directly contributing to your bottom line.
  • Levels the Playing Field: Our platform gives you access to the same kind of automation technology that large corporations use, helping you compete more effectively.

Cons & Considerations

  • Not for Complex Enterprises: Our platform is specifically designed for SMBs. It lacks the deep, custom-coded integrations and complex features required by very large enterprises.
  • Relies on Platform Uptime: Since we host and manage the agents, their operation is dependent on our platform's reliability (though we strive for 99.9% uptime).
  • Limited to Pre-defined Integrations: While our integration list is extensive for SMBs (especially with Zapier), you cannot build a completely novel, custom-coded integration from scratch.
  • AI is a Tool, Not Magic: The agent is only as good as the information you provide it. Getting the best results requires taking the time to train it properly with your business knowledge.
